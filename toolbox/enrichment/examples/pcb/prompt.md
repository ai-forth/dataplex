Build an engineering brief for the current catalog artifact using the available
evidence from schematics, BOM notes, manufacturing instructions, validation
plans, revision notes, and simulation material.

Prioritize information that helps another engineer answer:

* What function does this artifact serve in the board?
* What evidence ties it to adjacent circuitry, constraints, or interfaces?
* What should be verified before this design is released to manufacturing?
* What unresolved risk, ambiguity, or missing evidence remains?

When sources support it, explicitly call out:

* power, clock, reset, and protection behavior
* connector or interface assumptions
* component selection rationale
* DFM, DFT, or assembly concerns
* simulation or validation cases that should exist

Do not invent electrical behavior, values, or manufacturing claims.
